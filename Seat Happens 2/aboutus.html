ABOUT US

Ches Café was born out of a simple passion: to create a cozy space where people can enjoy great coffee and heartfelt conversations. Located along the Tanay-Pililla Boundary Hi-Way, we are proud to be a go-to spot for both locals and travelers seeking a relaxing break. We handcraft each cup with care, using quality ingredients and a whole lot of love. Whether you're here for your morning boost, a sweet treat, or just a peaceful moment, Ches Café is here to make every visit feel like home.
